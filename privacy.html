Privacy Policy for Medistock Viewer

Effective Date: July 08, 2026

Welcome to Medistock Viewer. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

To improve our software, ensure proper authentication, and understand our user base, we collect the following basic administrative information directly provided by you within the app:

  • Email Address: We collect the Gmail ID used to sign in to verify your identity and track daily usage.
  • Name of the Institution
  • Name of the Pharmacist
  • Name of the Countersigned Officer

2. How We Use Google Drive Data (Crucial)

Medistock Viewer requests access to your Google Drive (specifically the drive.file scope) strictly to allow YOU to backup and restore your medicine stock data securely to your own personal Google Drive account.

  • What we DO NOT collect: We do NOT read, store, intercept, or share your actual medicine stock data, inventory, bills, or any other files from your Google Drive.
  • Your backup file is stored locally on your device and inside your own Google Drive. Our servers do not have access to this backup file.

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited administrative information we collect (Email ID, Institution Name, Pharmacist Name, Officer Name) is logged securely and used solely for:

  • Authenticating users and preventing unauthorized access.
  • Understanding the demographics of institutions using our software.
  • Tracking daily usage statistics (who is using the app and when) to improve software performance.
  • Ensuring proper functionality for official medical record keeping.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, email address, or Google data to any third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may contact the developer of this application.
