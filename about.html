Welcome to Medistock Viewer

Medistock Viewer is a dedicated inventory management system designed specifically to streamline medicine stock handling and track medicine expiry dates accurately. It is built to serve the administrative needs of medical institutions, helping pharmacists organize inventory efficiently across Main Stock, Sub Stock OP, and Sub Stock IP divisions.

To ensure data security and prevent loss of critical medical records, this software requires users to authenticate via Google. It requests permission to connect to Google Drive strictly to allow users to securely backup their local daily stock entries directly to their own personal Google Drive account.
